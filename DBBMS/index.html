About Us

The Blood Bank was created as a platform for those who can donate. The bank's website acts as a central portal which provides information about what donating blood entails and tracking the number of people who register to become donors and eventually donate.


Our platform equally serves as a means for donors to connect and stay updated with local hospitals which may be in critical need for blood or simply as an means to view blood requests from hospitals in need.
